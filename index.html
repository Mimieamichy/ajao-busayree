By His Grace

2nd November 2025 · The Call

Imam Onireke told Ahmed her father would call him. True to his word, he did — and afterward sent Najeebah's number. Before anything else, the two agreed to perform Istikharah together, seeking Allah's guidance and leaving their fate in His hands.

Getting To Know Each Other

While observing the Istikharah, they continued their conversations. Alhamdulillah, the outcome was clear and good.

First Meeting · Lavish

The moment Ahmed saw her, he knew: "this is exactly my spec." Najeebah arrived with a sheet of carefully written questions for him — adorable, unforgettable, and, as it turned out, decisive. It was his honesty that settled things.

Three Months Later · Introduction

Since their introduction ceremony, Allah has made the journey easier than they ever imagined — every step filled with His mercy, blessings, and guidance.

15th August 2026 · Halal, Insha Allah

With grateful hearts, they now look forward to the day everything becomes halal. Alhamdulillahi Rabbil 'Alamin.
